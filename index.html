About Me

Hello! I am Francisco Buitron, a 22-year-old student from Irvine, California. I have lived in Irvine for almost 13 years, attending high school locally at University High School. Previously, I had lived in Temecula. I attended UCSB for my undergraduate degree in Biological Sciences. My hobbies include playing the guitar, piano, and basketball on the weekends. I love to travel, make and listen to music, and watch sports. My favorite teams are the Indianapolis Colts and the Los Angeles Lakers. I speak fluent Spanish and I hope to learn new languages, both spoken and coding.
